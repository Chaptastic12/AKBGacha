Battle system

Team of 3 v 1 AI boss


Each card has a Center Move, Backup Move, as well as a Team Leader skill if they are assigned the team leader slot.
Each card has a base Health [Stamina 体力], Attack [Determination 決心] and Defense [Kanjou 感情]
Each character will have their own health and will be knocked out when it drops to zero. Items will exist to reinvigorate them.
Each character will have a 'type' associated with them

Boss will be another Aidol with beefed up stats and skill set unique to that card.

User will be able to create and set their team based off of cards that they've summoned. Leader skills will be applied to that team.
